Cricket: The Gentlemen's Game

Cricket is a popular outdoor game played with bat and ball between two teams of eleven players each. The game is widely acknowledged as the game of gentlemen and played in over 100 countries across the globe.Cricket has a rich history and has evolved over time to become one of the most loved sports. It requires skills such as batting, bowling, and fielding and is often played in a large open field called a cricket ground.

Interesting facts

  1. The longest recorded cricket match lasted 14 days.
  2. Sachin Tendulkar played for Pakistan once!
  3. Iftikhar Ali Khan Pataudi is the only cricketer to have played Test cricket for both India and England.
